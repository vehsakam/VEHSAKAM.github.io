About

I'm Galymzhan

My name is Galymzhan Makashev, and I am currently finishing my bachelor studies at Nazarbayev University, majoring in Political Science and International Relations. Throughout my studies I took a variety of courses outside the limits of my major, most of which were from history and anthropology spheres. This helped me to develop a miscellaneous academic background, thanks to which I have a good expertise in my main research interest, which is nationalism and identity studies. Apart from that, I have an experience of research assistance with one of my professors on the project directly related to the notion of identity in Post-Soviet space in the view of Russo-Ukranian war.

Outside the academic field, I also dedicated 5 years to competitive debating. Owing to my international and local experience, I built competence in public speaking, argumentation, and presentation skills. Among my achievements there are:
- Victories at such international tournaments, as Nazarbayev University Debate Academy 2019 and Kultegin 2023
- Finals at numerous local tournaments, including KIMEP Cup 2019 and ENU Cup 2019

Aside from speaking career, I was also a Chair of the Educational Department at my debating club, and Coordinator of the Debating Community of Astana. This experience significantly developed my leadership, communication and management skills.
Thanks to such background, I also built an ambitious and empathetic personality, which combined with my major research interest led me to the path I am now sticking to. More precisely, I am looking forward to continue my studies at GSPP school at NU, which I believe will help me to achieve my goal of becoming an expert in governance and contribute into building an inclusive national identity in my home country.
